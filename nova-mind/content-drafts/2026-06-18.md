### Twitter/X 版

**推文：**

Self-evolving agents aren't sci-fi anymore.

A new survey (XMUDeepLIT/Awesome-Self-Evolving-Agents) classifies them into 3 paradigms:

1. **Model-centric** (evolve reasoning/training)
2. **Environment-centric** (evolve knowledge/experience/topology)
3. **Co-evolution** (both)

The key insight? The *environment* itself should evolve—not just the agent. Dynamic skill libraries, adaptive curricula, and self-reorganizing agent teams.

This is the blueprint for building agents that don't just learn, but *grow*.
